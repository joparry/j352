The Problem With Student Loans in America, Over The Years And Through the States

By Jack Parry

When looking deep into the data of complaints filed against Navient Solutions, LLC. on the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's website, I immediately noticed a large spike in complaints at a certain time period. In the studentloanplanner.com article, I read that the lawsuits started to come in during 2017, but I didn't realize just the sheer amount of complaints that were filed at that time as seen in the graph below. The number of complaints filed between 2017 and 2018 were a little less than all of the other years combined.

I also did a little digging on the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau website and was able to look at the states who filed lawsuits initially in 2017-2018. It made sense that California were in the fight, as it was the state that has filed the most student loan complaints to the CFPB over the last ten years. The other states seemed like they just wanted to go on the offensive against malpratices by Navient.
